ACADEMICS

Anju Gill Academy

Academics

The academy uses a comprehensive health-based education framework that is based upon CBSE curriculum. The framework is shown below in the diagram. The diagram shows an S.M.A.R.T. health-based K-12 school education framework for integrating healthful living standard course of study into the academic curriculum. As shown in the diagram, in order to develop various skills and knowledge, an integrated K-12 school course curriculum will be based upon S.M.A.R.T. healthful and academic goals. The school will use the S.M.A.R.T. (Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Realistic, and Time-bound) framework to set its goals and activities. The S.M.A.R.T. framework is an effective way to set and achieve individual as well as team goals.

PRE-PRIMARY SCHOOL PROGRAM

The pre-primary curriculum is designed to develop important skills in young children. The course-topics include:
  • Fine motor activities
  • Large motor activities
  • Numerical activities
  • Pre-reading activities
  • Reading activities
  • Writing activities
  • Sing along activities
  • Story sessions
  • Creativity
  • Healthful living

PRIMARY SCHOOL PROGRAM

The primary school curriculum has been designed based upon the program curriculum framework shown on the right. Integrating multiple perspectives into the course design helps students to see the world in all its complexity, not just from the point of view of one culture, discipline, field of study, or way of thinking. The content of each course will be designed using four perspectives-scientific, social, personal, and ethical perspectives. Reading, writing, speaking, and doing activities will be emphasized in every course. The courses will cover the following areas of learning:
  • Languages
  • Social Science
  • Sciences
  • Mathematics
  • Performing Arts
  • Healthful Living
The main courses in the primary school will be as follows:
2 courses in Languages
1 course in Social Science
1 course in Science
1 course in Mathematics
1 course in Performing arts
1 course in Physical Education and healthful Living
