ADMISSION

AGA seeks students who possess enthusiasm for learning, strong ability, and strong character in order to thrive in an academically challenging program. It is important to keep in mind that there is no formula for gaining admission to AGA. Admission to the academy is based strictly on merit. The AGA admission process is applied uniformly to every student irrespective of their cast, religion, and status. AGA admission committees select qualified students who are more than willing to engage not only in academic areas but also in a variety of other areas such as leadership, service, and athleticism.


The admission committees consider carefully students’ readiness to the program. Various assessments such as testing, academic records, observations, interviews, and teacher-recommendations are used by the admission committee while evaluating each applicant for admission. Social and behavioral maturity, and respect for others is also considered during the admission process.


Admission Process
  • Parents are required to fill the registration form.
  • The appropriate admission committee considers the test scores and other aspects of student profile. Parents will be given admission form if their respective student has passed the test and other criteria.
  • Parents submit the admission form within 3 days of the admission results.
  • Students scoring less on the entrance test are not considered for admission.
  • A borderline student is evaluated further only after receiving a certain minimum score as pre-established by the committee.
  • The final admission decision is taken by the main AGA committee consisting of professionals in USA.
  • A student is allowed to take the entrance test once. No student can take the entrance test again.
  • The admission form of the admitted student is accepted along with the documents and fees.
  • Mode of fees payment shall be in Cheque / Cash / DD /DEBIT card.

Entry criteria for admission
  • Admissions are granted to students based on the following assessments:
    • Nursery -Upper KG: Interaction of the child with the admission committee.
    • Class I onward: Written test followed by an interaction with the admission committee.
  • Student Viva is considered an important input to the admission committee for admission.
  • Concept and entrance tests are based on expected key milestones for every class
  • Any student seeking admission to Std 9th in Anju Gill Academy shall undergo an interest inventory test in addition to other tests.
  • Students of Anju Gill Academy need not give any entrance test or assessment in case of internal transfers.
Documents to be submitted at the time of admission
  • Medical Fitness certificate
  • Attested copy of birth certificate of the child
  • Progress card of previous academic year( std I onwards) of the child
  • Previous school’s leaving certificate/ Transfer Certificate (if any)
  • Aadhar Card copy of the student and parents
  • 4 copies of Passport size photograph of a child, mother & father.
