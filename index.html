ANJU GILL ACADEMY

Surya Text Bhan Trust has established Anju Gill Academy (AGA) as a non-profit English-medium K-12 school. AGA is supported by U.S.A. based educational professionals. The school is a source of quality education with added emphasis on healthful and hygienic living aspects. It is a co-educational school.


The admission to the academy will be on merit basis. It is our vision that 10% of admissions at this academy will go to deserving pupils whose families are poor and unable to afford education.



AGA’s core philosophy is expressed through integrity, mutual respect, pursuit of excellence, creativity, and innovation.


The academy uses a comprehensive health-based education framework that is based upon CBSE curriculum. The framework is shown below in the diagram. The diagram shows an S.M.A.R.T. health-based K-12 school education framework for integrating healthful living standard course of study into the academic curriculum. As shown in the diagram, in order to develop various skills and knowledge, an integrated K-12 school course curriculum will be based upon S.M.A.R.T. healthful and academic goals. The school will use the S.M.A.R.T. (Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Realistic, and Time-bound) framework to set its goals and activities. The S.M.A.R.T. framework is an effective way to set and achieve individual as well as team goals.

Vision

AGA will provide that education by which a child develops character of honor, integrity, and self-confidence. We strive to produce youth who contribute to forming a healthy and virtuous community and nation. AGA will have emphasis on developing innovative mind and independent thinkers.
Mission

AGA aims to develop students who:

  • Mature intellectually, emotionally, socially, physically, and aesthetically
  • Practice personal wellness
  • Develop a passion for learning beyond school
  • Take responsibility and develop independent mind
  • Challenge their intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, multiple perspectives, and problem solving
  • Would be encouraged to develop shared dialog through collaboration

PRINCIPAL’S MESSAGE
Principal’s Welcome …

Please allow me to extend a warm welcome to everyone on behalf of Anju Gill Academy (AGA). Our mission is to provide exceptional education opportunities for children to grow and learn in a stimulating and nurturing environment. That is the purpose of creating AGA. The school will endeavor to inculcate a stimulative learning experience. Our desire is to make AGA a unique place of learning. The management’s focus is geared to learning, exploration, and creativity so that the child can achieve well-rounded education.


Our Motto of Excellence in Education and All Round Development of our children is diligently pursued by providing a positive learning environment in which resources are channelized to achieve the maximum development of the physical, intellectual, social and spiritual aspects of our children’s personality. We aim to instill in them a shared perception of life which will draw them closer to their peers elsewhere, thus promoting a peaceful and harmonious environment where everyone can co-exist irrespective of caste, creed, and culture.


We pray that Almighty God would bless our children with good health, wisdom, knowledge, understanding and success in whatever they do.
