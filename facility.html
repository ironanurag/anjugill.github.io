Facility

AGA is an educational institution that prioritizes student learning and engagement through the use of advanced teaching technologies. The school features well-equipped classrooms with projectors that provide an immersive learning experience to students. These projectors enable the presentation of complex concepts and ideas through text, images, and videos in a way that is easier for students to comprehend.


AGA's laboratories are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and are used to teach a range of subjects such as English, mathematics, physics, chemistry, and biology. The laboratory approach to teaching allows students to gain first-hand experience and practice critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Additionally, AGA's language laboratories provide an ideal environment for students to improve their fluency and confidence in a second language.

The use of smart boards in several laboratories at AGA is an innovative addition to the teaching environment. These interactive whiteboards provide teachers with a variety of tools to enhance the learning experience, including digital pen tools, annotation features, and multimedia integration. The use of smart boards promotes active learning and encourages student participation, providing a collaborative learning environment.


In conclusion, AGA is an institution that is committed to providing students with a high-quality education through the use of advanced teaching technologies. The integration of projectors, laboratories, and smart boards enhances the learning experience by providing students with practical knowledge and skills that can be applied in their academic and professional pursuits.
