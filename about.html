About us

Surya Text Bhan Trust has established Anju Gill Academy (AGA) as a non-profit English-medium K-12 school. AGA is supported by U.S.A. based educational professionals. The school is a source of quality education with added emphasis on healthful and hygienic living aspects. It is a co-educational school.



The admission to the academy will be on merit basis. It is our vision that 10% of admissions at this academy will go to deserving pupils whose families are poor and unable to afford education.

The S.M.A.R.T. framework is an effective way to set and achieve individual as well as team goals.

Vision

AGA will provide that education by which a child develops character of honor, integrity, and self-confidence. We strive to produce youth who contribute to forming a healthy and virtuous community and nation. AGA will have emphasis on developing innovative mind and independent thinkers.
Mission

AGA aims to develop students who:

  • Mature intellectually, emotionally, socially, physically, and aesthetically
  • Practice personal wellness
  • Develop a passion for learning beyond school
  • Take responsibility and develop independent mind
  • Challenge their intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, multiple perspectives, and problem solving
  • Would be encouraged to develop shared dialog through collaboration
